How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Gather Ingredients
    Collect bread, your choice of fillings (e.g., cheese, ham, veggies), and condiments (e.g., mayo, mustard).
  2. Lay Out Two Slices of Bread
    Place the bread slices on a clean surface, such as a plate or cutting board.
  3. Spread Condiments
    Use a knife to spread your preferred condiments, like mayonnaise or mustard, onto one or both slices of bread.
  4. Add Protein
    Place your chosen protein (e.g., slices of deli meat, grilled chicken, or tuna salad) on one slice of bread.
  5. Layer Vegetables
    Add vegetables like lettuce, tomato, cucumber, or onions on top of the protein.
  6. Add Cheese
    Place a slice or two of cheese on top of the vegetables, if desired.
  7. Top With the Second Slice of Bread
    Place the second slice of bread on top to close the sandwich.
  8. Cut and Serve
    Use a knife to cut the sandwich into halves or quarters and enjoy!
